cancer.03 - group of diseases involving abnormal cell growth

NOTES: Added for THYME-AMR.

Aliases:

cancer (n.)

Roles:

ARG1-PPT: patient
ARG2-LOC: primary organ
ARG3-LOC: specific part of organ, if in addition to ARG2
ARG4-LOC: cell location/field (e.g., squamous cell; urothelium)
